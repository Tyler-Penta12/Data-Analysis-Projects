## Projects

### 📊 Analyzing Website Performance for the Grammys
**Tools:** Excel, PivotTables, SUMIFS, AVERAGEIFS, XLOOKUP
- Compared user engagement KPIs across Grammy.com and RecordingAcademy.com to assess the business impact of their 2022 website split
- Built an interactive dashboard with KPI cards and custom visuals

### 🧪 A/B Testing Warby Parker's Landing Page
**Tools:** Excel, PivotTables
- Analyzed 3,500+ user sessions across device types, regions, and age groups
- Found the redesigned landing page produced a 14.9% conversion rate with users under 25 responding most positively

### ❄️ Winter Sports Interest Analysis
**Tools:** Excel, Google Trends
- Analyzed search interest for 9 Olympic sports from 2006–2022
- Identified Bobsleigh as the highest average interest sport among lesser-known events

### 🚗 DoorDash Delivery Analysis
**Tools:** Excel, PivotTables
- Analyzed 50,000+ orders across New York City, Chicago, San Francisco, and Seattle
- Average delivery time was 49.9 minutes with a standard deviation of 21.6 minutes

### 📈 Regression Analysis of Income & Education (NLSY97)
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Statsmodels, Matplotlib, Jupyter Notebook
- Performed univariate regression on 8,900+ individuals finding each additional year of education significantly increases annual income (p < 0.05)
- Created binary variables for gender and college completion to identify key income predictors
